About Us

Lotto & Animal Similarity Test is a high-quality web platform dedicated to providing useful tools and interactive experiences.

Our Mission

We aim to combine probability-based lottery number generation with modern artificial intelligence (AI) to create fun and reliable services for our users.

Technology

Our Lotto Generator uses standard mathematical randomization, and our Animal Face Test is powered by Google's Teachable Machine and TensorFlow.js, ensuring high performance and privacy by processing images directly in your browser.
